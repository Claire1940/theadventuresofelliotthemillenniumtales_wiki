<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Faie Magic and Local Co-op Setup">

Faie is Elliot’s fairy companion and serves as a vital tool for both combat and puzzle-solving. By default, the single player can control Faie independently using the right analog stick on the Xbox controller. This allows you to scout ahead, retrieve distant items, or trigger switches that Elliot cannot reach.

The game also supports local 1-2 player co-op. In this mode, a second player can join the game on the same Xbox console, taking direct control of Faie using a second controller. This cooperative dynamic changes how you approach exploration and combat, as Faie can cast powerful support magic while Elliot focuses on melee engagement.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Connect a Second Controller">
    Turn on a second Xbox Wireless Controller and sign in to a guest account or a secondary Xbox profile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Open the Pause Menu">
    Launch the game, load your active save file, and press the Menu button on the primary controller to open the pause screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Enable Co-op Mode">
    Navigate to the multiplayer settings option and select Enable Local Co-op. The second player will instantly control Faie.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assign Faie Magic">
    Equip Faie's active magic spells (Warp, Ignite, Vacuum, or Copy) in the party menu to coordinate your co-op strategies.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Co-op Combat Synergy">
When playing in local co-op, have the second player use Faie's Vacuum magic to pull a group of enemies together. Once they are clustered, Elliot can immediately follow up with a heavy Hammer attack or place a Bomb to clear the entire group instantly.
</Callout>

Faie's magic is unlocked by visiting Shrines of the Mystic hidden throughout the world. Each magic type has specific applications for both solving environmental puzzles and defeating enemies:

| Magic Type | Primary Effect | Exploration Use | Combat Use |

| Warp | Teleports Elliot to Faie | Crosses large gaps and hazards | Repositions behind boss shields |
| Ignite | Lights objects and melts ice | Solves torch puzzles | Detonates placed combat bombs |
| Vacuum | Pulls enemies and items close | Gathers distant loot drops | Groups small enemies for AOE |
| Copy | Mirrors Elliot's actions | Solves synchronized switches | Doubles projectile attack paths |
