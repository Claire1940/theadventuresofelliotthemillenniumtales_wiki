<Section icon={Shield} title="Magicite Customization and Combat Builds">

Magicite customization serves as the backbone of character progression. By exploring dungeons, completing sidequests, and defeating bosses, you will collect magicite fragments that can be slotted into weapon-specific Magicite Boxes. This system allows you to tailor your loadout to match your preferred playstyle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Gather Magicite Fragments">
    Search the environment, break clay pots, open chests, and defeat elite monsters across different eras to find raw magicite fragments.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Magicite Box">
    Open your equipment menu on Steam and navigate to your active weapons. Each weapon features dedicated slots where magicite can be placed.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Slot and Sync Passives">
    Match offensive, defensive, or utility magicite to your active weapons. For example, slotting a guard-focused magicite into your shield will enhance your stamina recovery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dismantle Duplicates">
    Utilize the automatic duplicate dismantling feature in the inventory menu to break down unneeded magicite into raw materials for further upgrades.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Following player feedback from the initial debut demo, Square Enix updated the user interface to include weapon-specific sorting, quick-equip loadouts, and streamlined magicite boxes, making build creation much smoother on PC.

<Callout type="success" title="Build Synergy">
Always align your magicite effects with your weapon's role. Putting critical-hit rate magicite on fast-hitting weapons like the Sword or Chains yields far better results than placing it on the slow, single-hit Hammer.
</Callout>
