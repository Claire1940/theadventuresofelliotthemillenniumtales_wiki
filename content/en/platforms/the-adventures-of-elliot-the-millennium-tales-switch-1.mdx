<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Strategy">

Beyond basic attacks, players must master the **Perfect Block**. Timing your block just as an enemy strike lands will negate all damage and often leave the opponent vulnerable to a counter-hit. This is especially important for the "Tag Guardians," who have specific patterns that can only be broken by well-timed defensive play.

<Section icon={Target} title="Faie Magic and Exploration Walkthrough">

Faie is much more than a cosmetic companion; she is a secondary character that can be controlled independently using the right stick. In local co-op mode, a second player can take full control of Faie to assist with combat and puzzles. Her magic is unlocked by visiting **Shrines of the Mystic** scattered across the four eras. These shrines grant abilities that are mandatory for progression through the game's more complex dungeons.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock Warp Magic">
    Visit the first Shrine of the Mystic to learn Warp. This allows Elliot to teleport to Faie's current location, bypassing pits and traps.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Scout with Faie">
    Use the independent control to send Faie into dangerous rooms. She can trigger switches or distract enemies before Elliot enters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master Ignite & Vacuum">
    Use Ignite to melt ice obstacles and Vacuum to pull distant loot or grouped enemies into your weapon's reach.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Synchronize with Copy">
    Later in the game, learn Copy to have Faie mimic your weapon actions, effectively doubling your damage output for a short duration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Exploration Tip">
Always look for "Adventuring Guideposts." Activating these allows for instant fast travel within the current era. Backtracking is a major part of the time-travel mechanic, so these guideposts save hours of travel time.
</Callout>

Exploration is divided into the "Four Ages": Safekeeping, Reconstruction, Magic, and Budding. Actions you take in an earlier era, such as planting a seed or moving a boulder, will directly affect the landscape and available sidequests in the future eras. This temporal puzzle-solving is a core pillar of the walkthrough.
