<Section icon={Map} title="Misr Ruins Location and Era Mechanics">

The Misr Ruins serve as one of the earliest complex dungeons in the game, challenging players to utilize the core time-travel mechanics. To locate and unlock every chest, you must understand how actions in one era affect the geography of the next. The ruins exist across multiple timelines, but your search will focus primarily on the **Age of Safekeeping** and the **Age of Reconstruction**.

When exploring the ruins in the Age of Safekeeping, you will find sturdy stone walls, active water channels, and intact bridges. In the Age of Reconstruction, centuries of decay have collapsed these structures, opening new pathways while blocking others with heavy rubble. Navigating this temporal layout is essential for reaching the most valuable treasure chests.

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Altar Chest Walkthrough">

The primary chest in the Misr Ruins is located in the Hidden Altar room. This chest is locked behind a dual-era puzzle that requires precise movement and timing. Follow these steps to complete the puzzle and claim your rewards.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Doorway of Time">
    Begin in the Age of Safekeeping. Navigate to the central hub of the Misr Ruins and locate the active Doorway of Time. Clear the surrounding skeleton enemies using your Sword and Shield counterattacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear the Reconstruction Rubble">
    Pass through the portal to enter the Age of Reconstruction. Walk to the eastern wing where a collapsed pillar blocks a narrow drainage pipe. Place a Bomb at the base of the pillar and detonate it using Faie's Ignite magic to clear the blockage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Return to the Age of Safekeeping">
    Step back through the Doorway of Time. Because you cleared the drainage pipe in the future, the water levels in the past have drained, revealing a hidden lower walkway in the western chamber of the ruins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Navigate the Western Altar Path">
    Descend into the newly revealed walkway. Equip your Boomerang and throw it at the glowing blue crystal switch across the spiked pit. This will lower the iron gate blocking the Hidden Altar chest room.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Retrieve the Altar Chest">
    Enter the altar room. The chest sits on a raised platform surrounded by a deep chasm. Direct Faie over the gap using the right stick, cast Vacuum magic to pull the chest toward the edge, and open it to claim your loot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Puzzle Solved">
Completing this sequence unlocks the Hidden Altar room permanently across all eras, allowing you to access the lower chambers of the ruins in later chapters of the story.
</Callout>
