<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Core Mechanics of the Magicite System">

In the HD-2D action RPG developed by Square Enix and Claytechworks, combat customization is built entirely around Magicite. These ancient, glowing shards are slotted directly into Elliot's weapons to alter their properties, boost base statistics, and trigger special combat effects. Unlike traditional RPG systems where stats are tied strictly to character levels, your combat efficiency in this game depends heavily on how you configure your weapon-specific Magicite Boxes.

The system is highly flexible, allowing you to swap Magicite configurations in real time to adapt to different eras, dungeons, and boss encounters. Following feedback from the debut demo survey, the developers implemented several quality-of-life improvements for the final release on June 18, 2026. These include a dedicated Magicite Box UI for each of the seven weapon types, advanced inventory sorting filters, and an automatic duplicate dismantling system that converts excess shards into upgrade materials.

| System Feature | Mechanic Description | Priority Rating |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Magicite Fragments** | Found in chests, dungeons, and breakable objects to socket into weapons | High |
| **Magicite Boxes** | Weapon-specific customization slots that restrict or enhance certain builds | High |
| **Auto-Dismantling** | Automatically breaks down duplicate magicites into useful upgrading materials | Medium |
| **Custom Sorting** | Filter magicites by weapon type, elemental effect, or stat boost | Medium |

To maximize your combat potential, you must collect Magicite fragments scattered across the four distinct historical eras: the Age of Safekeeping, the Age of Reconstruction, the Age of Magic, and the Age of Budding. Slotting a Magicite does not just offer passive stat boosts; it can fundamentally change how a weapon's special attacks behave. For example, a standard slash can be transformed into a ranged projectile wave, or a heavy hammer blow can trigger localized shockwaves.

<Callout type="info" title="Magicite Box Restrictions">
Each weapon has a limited number of Magicite slots. Some slots are locked behind specific weapon upgrade levels, while others are restricted to certain elemental or utility types. Always plan your build around the available slots of your primary and secondary weapons.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Weapon-Specific Builds and Magicite Synergies">

With seven distinct weapon types available, creating a cohesive build requires understanding how Magicite interacts with weapon mechanics and Elliot's companion, Faie. Because Elliot can equip two weapons at once and swap them in real time during combat, the most effective builds use a primary damage-dealing weapon paired with a utility-focused secondary weapon.

| Primary Weapon | Secondary Weapon | Synergistic Magicite Strategy | Recommended Build |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sword & Shield** | Bow | Guard-focused sword magicite paired with ranged damage openers | Balanced Melee |
| **Bomb** | Boomerang | Delayed burst magicite paired with returning projectile setups | Utility Burst |
| **Spear** | Hammer | Reach-extending spear magicite combined with heavy-hit hammer slots | Specialist Combat |
| **Chains & Sickles** | Bomb | Crowd-control pull magicite paired with high-impact bomb explosions | Advanced Control |

### The Balanced Melee Build (Sword & Shield + Bow)
This build is the safest starter option for general exploration. Slotting the *Counter Stance* or *Brave* Magicite into your Sword and Shield ensures high melee damage during close-quarters engagements. Meanwhile, equipping your Bow with utility Magicite allows you to trigger switches, hit distant flying enemies, and apply status effects before transitioning back to melee range.

### The Ignite Combo Build (Bomb + Boomerang)
This advanced setup relies heavily on cooperation with Faie. By slotting explosion-focused Magicite into your Bomb loadout, you increase the blast radius and damage of your explosives. Use the Boomerang to herd enemies together, drop a bomb, and then have Faie use her *Ignite* magic to detonate the explosives instantly, bypassing the standard delay timer.

<Callout type="tip" title="Fairy Magic Integration">
Faie's support abilities, such as *Vacuum* and *Copy*, can be enhanced by specific Magicite builds. Slotting projectile-expanding Magicite into your Boomerang or Bow allows Faie's *Copy* magic to replicate multiple projectiles, filling the screen with damaging hitboxes.
</Callout>
