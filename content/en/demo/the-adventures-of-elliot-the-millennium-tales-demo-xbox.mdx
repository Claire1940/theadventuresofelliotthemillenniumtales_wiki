<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Faie Magic and Local Co-op Mechanics">

Elliot is accompanied on his journey by Faie, a glowing fairy companion who provides vital support during both combat and puzzle-solving. On Xbox, Player 1 controls Elliot using the left analog stick, while the right analog stick can be used to independently maneuver Faie around the screen.

The game also features full local 1-2 player co-op. A second player can activate a controller to take direct control of Faie. This turns the game into a cooperative tactical experience, where one player handles the frontline swordplay while the other scouts ahead, gathers items, and casts support magic.

| Faie Spell | How to Unlock | Primary Combat & Puzzle Effect | Best Cooperative Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warp** | Shrine of the Mystic | Teleports Elliot instantly to Faie's location | Escape boss traps & cross wide chasms |
| **Ignite** | Opening Story Event | Lights torches, melts ice, detonates bombs | Trigger remote explosives on command |
| **Vacuum** | Hidden dungeon shrine | Pulls items, loot, and small enemies together | Group enemies for heavy hammer attacks |
| **Copy** | Mid-demo side quest | Mimics Elliot's movements and projectile actions | Activate dual-switch puzzles simultaneously |

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastering the Warp Spell">
Faie's Warp is your most valuable tool for safety. If Elliot is about to be hit by a boss's unblockable area-of-effect attack, send Faie to a safe corner of the arena and trigger Warp to instantly escape danger.
</Callout>

In addition to her active spells, Faie can revive Elliot in battle by spending "tul," the game's primary currency. This makes her presence indispensable during challenging boss fights, such as the encounter with the Tag Guardians in the Doorway Ruins.
