<Section icon={Target} title="Magicite Customization and Build Strategies">

Magicite fragments are collectible items found throughout the four eras that can be slotted into weapon-specific Magicite Boxes. This customization system allows you to alter weapon behavior, boost core stats, and introduce passive effects. Square Enix has also introduced a robust save-data bonus campaign, rewarding players who have played previous Square Enix titles with exclusive sword magicite.

| Eligible Game Save Data | Magicite Reward | Special Combat Effect |
|---|---|---|
| Octopath Traveler 0 | Guidance of the Sacred Flame | +10% sword damage to enemies below 30% HP |
| Dragon Quest I & II Remake | Critical Hit | Increases sword critical-hit rate by 6% |
| Bravely Default Flying Fairy | Brave | +10% sword damage, increases shield stamina cost |
| Various Daylife | Expeditionary Wisdom | Reduces sword charge attack time by 10% |
| Triangle Strategy | Counter Stance | +10% sword damage when shield stamina is above 90% |
| Trials of Mana | Laurentian Plumage | Ornamental item that triggers a special town encounter |

<Section icon={Shield} title="Faie Magic and Co-op Exploration">

Faie is not merely a story companion; she is a core gameplay pillar. Players can control Faie independently using the right analog stick, or a second player can take control of her in local 1-2 player co-op mode. Faie uses unique magical abilities learned at Shrines of the Mystic to assist with combat, puzzles, and traversal.

| Magic Type | Combat Function | Puzzle & Exploration Use |
|---|---|---|
| Warp | Repositions Elliot instantly | Crosses wide gaps and bypasses traps |
| Ignite | Detonates bombs and ignites foes | Melts ice blocks and lights torches |
| Vacuum | Pulls enemies into a tight group | Collects dropped loot and distant keys |
| Copy | Mimics Elliot's primary actions | Triggers synchronized pressure plates |
