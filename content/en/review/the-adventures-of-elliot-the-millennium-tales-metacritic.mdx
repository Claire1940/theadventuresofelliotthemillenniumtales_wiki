<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Faie Magic and Local Co-op Strategy">

Faie is not merely a narrative companion; she is a core gameplay pillar controlled independently using the right analog stick. She can scout dangerous pathways, retrieve out-of-reach items, and cast specialized support magic learned from Shrines of the Mystic. 

Furthermore, local 1-2 player support allows a second player to assume direct control of Faie, turning the adventure into a fully cooperative dungeon-crawling experience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Position Faie Across Gaps">
    Use the right analog stick to fly Faie across gaps, over pressure plates, or behind enemy lines while Elliot remains in a safe position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Cast Warp Magic">
    Activate Warp Magic learned from the Shrine of the Mystic to instantaneously teleport Elliot to Faie's exact location, bypassing hazards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Surprise Attacks">
    Teleport directly behind shielded or distracted enemies to initiate high-damage backstrikes or escape tight corners.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Faie's magical repertoire expands as you explore the world and discover hidden shrines. Each spell offers distinct advantages that can be combined with Elliot's weapon attacks for powerful combat synergies.
