Faie is Elliot's fairy companion, offering essential support during combat, puzzle-solving, and map navigation. She can be controlled independently using the right analog stick, or a second player can take control of her locally for a seamless 1-2 player cooperative experience.

As you explore shrines throughout the world, Faie will learn powerful magic spells that completely alter how you interact with the environment and enemies.

| Magic Type | Combat Effect | Exploration Utility | Best Weapon Pairing | Unlock Method |
| Warp Magic | Teleports Elliot to Faie | Crosses gaps and hazards | Hammer, Sword and Shield | Shrine of the Mystic |

| Vacuum Magic | Draws enemies together | Collects distant loot items | Chains and Sickles, Bomb | Shrine of the Mystic |
| Copy Magic | Mirrors weapon actions | Solves synchronized switches | Boomerang, Bow | Shrine of the Mystic |

When playing in local co-op, communication is key. Player two can scout ahead into dangerous rooms, trigger switches from afar, and position themselves perfectly to allow player one to warp out of danger or execute devastating surprise attacks.
