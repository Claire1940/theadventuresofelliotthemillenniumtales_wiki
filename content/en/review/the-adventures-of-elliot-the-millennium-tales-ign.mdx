<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Faie Magic and Local Co-op Tactics">

Faie is Elliot's fairy companion and serves as a vital tool for both combat and exploration. She can be controlled independently using the right analog stick, allowing players to scout dangerous paths ahead before committing Elliot to a hazardous route. Additionally, the game supports local 1-2 player co-op on supported platforms, allowing a second player to take full control of Faie.

As you explore the game's diverse eras, visiting Shrines of the Mystic unlocks powerful magic spells for Faie, transforming her from a simple scout into a devastating support partner.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate a Shrine of the Mystic">
    Search the world map across different eras to find hidden Shrines of the Mystic. These ancient structures are the only locations where Faie can acquire new magical abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unlock Warp Magic">
    Interact with the shrine to learn Warp Magic. This core ability allows Elliot to instantly teleport to Faie's current position, bypassing dangerous terrain and hazards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Position Faie Independently">
    Use the right stick (or have Player 2 control her) to send Faie across gaps, behind enemy lines, or onto elevated ledges that Elliot cannot normally reach.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Warp teleport">
    Activate the Warp ability to pull Elliot directly to Faie. Use this sequence to solve intricate environmental puzzles, escape enemy surrounds, or execute surprise drop attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>

In addition to Warp, Faie can master several other spells that synergize with Elliot's weapon loadouts:
- **Ignite Magic**: Lights torches, melts icy obstacles, and detonates deployed bombs instantly for burst damage.
- **Vacuum Magic**: Draws nearby enemies and dropped loot items together, making crowd control and item collection effortless.
- **Copy Magic**: Mirrors Elliot's actions, allowing Faie to trigger synchronized switches or double the coverage of projectile attacks.

<Callout type="info" title="Co-op Synchronization">
During local co-op play, communication is key. Coordinate Faie's Vacuum Magic to group enemies together just as Elliot unleashes a charged Hammer or Sword attack for maximum damage output.
</Callout>
