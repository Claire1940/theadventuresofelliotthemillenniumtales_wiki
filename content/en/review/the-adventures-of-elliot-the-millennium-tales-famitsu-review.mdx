<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Faie's Magical Abilities and Co-op Gameplay">

Faie is Elliot's fairy companion and serves as a vital asset during exploration and combat. Players can control Faie independently using the right analog stick, or a second local player can take direct control of her in local 1-2 player cooperative mode. This dual-character setup adds a unique layer of coordination to the gameplay.

As you explore the world across four distinct ages (Safekeeping, Reconstruction, Magic, and Budding), you will discover Shrines of the Mystic. These shrines grant Faie powerful magical spells that alter how you interact with the environment and engage enemies.

| Magic Type | Core Effect | Exploration Use | Combat Synergy |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Warp | Teleports Elliot to Faie | Crosses wide gaps and hazard traps | Repositions behind enemies instantly |
| Ignite | Lights torches and melts ice | Solves environmental puzzle doors | Detonates combat bombs remotely |
| Vacuum | Pulls enemies and items in | Gathers dropped loot and materials | Groups targets for heavy hammer swings |
