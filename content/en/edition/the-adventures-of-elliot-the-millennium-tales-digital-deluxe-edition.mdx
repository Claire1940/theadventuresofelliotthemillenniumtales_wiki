Magicite is a core upgrade material used to change weapon behavior, stats, and combat effects. If you have save data from other specific Square Enix HD-2D or RPG titles on your platform, you can claim these powerful modifiers.

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Guide: How to Claim Deluxe Items">

Whether you are trying to acquire your Digital Deluxe Pack accessories, your preorder bonuses, or your save data Magicite, the process is identical. The game requires you to manually link the bonuses and then physically collect them in the game world.

Follow these steps to ensure your items are successfully transferred into your active inventory.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Promotional Bonus Menu">
    Launch the game and remain on the main Title Screen. Navigate to the **Promotional Bonus** option in the menu. This system checks your account entitlements for the Digital Deluxe Upgrade and scans your local storage for eligible save data.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Link Your Eligible Content">
    Follow the on-screen prompts to link your Deluxe Edition entitlements and any eligible game saves. The system will confirm which bonuses have been successfully registered to your current profile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Start or Load Your Game">
    Begin a new game or load your existing save file. If you are starting fresh, you must complete the opening tutorial sequence and progress the story until you reach the Age of Safekeeping.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Visit the Orphanage">
    Once you have free roam in the Age of Safekeeping, navigate to the local orphanage. Head up the stairs to the second floor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Interact with the Desk">
    Locate the specific desk on the second floor of the orphanage. Interact with it to open the reward cache. All linked items, including the Fairy Bangle, Cherry Blossom Anklet, Roselle Ring, and any Magicite, will be deposited into your inventory.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Demo Save Transfers">
If you played the Prologue Demo, your progress carries over to the full game. However, linked save data bonuses and Digital Deluxe items cannot be redeemed inside the demo itself. You must use the full retail version to interact with the orphanage desk and claim your rewards.
</Callout>
