Once you have unboxed your physical edition and redeemed your Digital Deluxe voucher code on your platform's store, you must retrieve the items in-game. Follow these steps to successfully link your accounts, scan for save files, and collect your rewards in-game.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Redeem the Deluxe Voucher Code">
    Input the 12-digit or 16-digit code found on the physical insert of your package into the PlayStation Store, Xbox Store, Nintendo eShop, or Steam client. Ensure the game is fully updated to the latest version before launching.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Scan for Promotional Bonuses">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Progress to the Age of Safekeeping">
    Launch your game and complete the introductory prologue tutorial. Once you gain full control of Elliot and Faie in the **Age of Safekeeping**, make your way to the local orphanage where Elliot's journey begins.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Retrieve Items from the Orphanage Desk">

  </Step>
</Steps>
